## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why isn't the music playing?**

A: Musical Siddur connects to the Apple Music catalog to play high-quality audio. You must have an active Apple Music subscription or authorization to play the media on your device. If you do not have a subscription, the audio features will not function.

**Q: Can I suggest a song?**

A: Yes! You can use the form linked above, or use the "Suggest" button inside the app (Long-press on any verse).

## Privacy Policy
**Last updated: Nov 30, 2025**

Musical Siddur respects your privacy. 

1. **No Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.
2. **No Tracking:** We do not use any third-party tracking or analytics software.
3. **Apple Music:** The app interacts with Apple's MusicKit framework. Any data regarding your music playback is handled directly between your device and Apple, subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your playback history.
4. **Support Inquiries:** If you contact us for support/to suggest songs, we will use the contact information you provide solely to reply to your inquiry. We do not sell or share contact information.
